Certified AI Professional

The Beingcert Certified Artificial Intelligence Professional certification exam is a global vendor-neutral certification that validates the knowledge and skills required to work in the Artificial Intelligence domain. This certification is ideal for students pursuing computer science or engineering degrees who want to gain a solid foundation in AI and its subfields as well as professionals working in the field of data science, machine learning, or AI to expand their knowledge and skills. The certification covers 6 domains including Introduction to AI, Math Fundamentals for AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning AI in Practice,6 Programming for AI.
